# ExamProject

## BI Problem Statement – Airbnb Price and Distance to City Center

### Context

Airbnb listings change in price depending on several factors such as location, room type, availability, and demand. One important factor that may influence the price is the distance from the city center. Airbnbs located closer to the city center are often more attractive to tourists due to easier access to attractions, restaurants, and public transportation.

### Goal

The goal of this analysis is to examine whether the distance from the city center influences the price of Airbnb listings. 

### Research Question

Does the distance from the city center affect the price of Airbnb listings?

### Hypotheses

* H₀ (Null Hypothesis):*
The distance from the city center does not affect the price of an Airbnb listing.

* H₁ (Alternative Hypothesis):*
Airbnb listings closer to the city center tend to have higher prices.
